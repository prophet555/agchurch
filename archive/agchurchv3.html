Welcome to AG Church

We are a community of believers who are passionate about following Jesus Christ. We believe that the Bible is the inspired word of God, and we are committed to living out its teachings in our everyday lives. We offer a variety of ministries and programs for all ages, and we welcome everyone to join us in our worship and fellowship.

Bible Study

We offer a variety of Bible studies for all ages and interests. Our studies are led by qualified teachers who are passionate about sharing the Word of God. We believe that studying the Bible is essential for growing in our faith, and we encourage everyone to join us in one of our studies.

Art

We believe that art is a powerful way to express our faith. We offer a variety of art classes and workshops for all ages and skill levels. We encourage everyone to use their creativity to worship God and share the gospel with others.

Mission

We are committed to sharing the gospel with the world. We support a variety of missionaries around the globe, and we also offer opportunities for our members to get involved in local missions. We believe that everyone has a part to play in reaching the world for Christ, and we encourage everyone to get involved in our mission work.

Financials

We are a non-profit organization, and we rely on the generous support of our members and friends to keep our ministry going. We are committed to being transparent with our finances, and we publish our annual report online. We appreciate your prayers and support as we continue to reach the world for Christ.
